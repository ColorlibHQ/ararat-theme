== Description ==
Ararat is a clean, simple, responsive and customizable WordPress theme that looks awesome on any device. For a quick launch of a page to showcases your works, your talents and your services, apredefined theme is a wise decision to make. Ararat has it all in store for you to easily and effortlessly engineer an appealing page to attract new clients and seal new business deals.
